
## Indoor Sports Ground — Professional Website

### Design & Theme
- **Green & White** color palette with clean, modern typography
- Dark accents for contrast and premium feel
- Smooth scroll animations (fade-in, slide-up) on all sections
- Fully responsive (mobile, tablet, desktop)

### Sections (Single-Page Layout)

1. **Hero Section** — Full-width banner with bold headline, tagline ("Your Premier Indoor Sports Destination"), and a CTA button linking to WhatsApp
2. **About / Description** — Indoor ground overview, what makes it special, operating hours
3. **Sports Offered** — Cricket & Football cards with icons and short descriptions
4. **Key Features** — Grid showcasing: Professional-grade turf, floodlights, changing rooms, parking, refreshments, etc.
5. **Gallery** — Image grid with hover effects (placeholder images initially)
6. **Pricing / Packages** — Simple pricing cards for hourly/session bookings
7. **Location & Map** — Embedded Google Maps iframe with address details
8. **Footer** — Contact info, social links, copyright

### WhatsApp Chat Button
- Floating green WhatsApp button (bottom-right corner)
- Clicking opens `https://wa.me/<number>` with a pre-filled message like "Hi, I'd like to inquire about booking"
- Placeholder number that you can easily update

### Animations
- Scroll-triggered fade-in animations for each section
- Hover effects on cards and gallery images
- Smooth navigation with anchor links
